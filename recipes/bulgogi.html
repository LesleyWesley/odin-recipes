Bulgogi

Description

An easy recipe for Korean beef bulgogi. Delicious served with rice or lettuce. If you want to get crazy, you can even use it as the filling of a cheesy quesadilla!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin, cut thin
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Steps

  1. Whisk all ingredients except steak and honey in a large bowl. Add steak and toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl and let marinate for 1 hour, or up to 1 day.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir steak and marinade together, adding honey to carmelize the steak. Cook until steak is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
