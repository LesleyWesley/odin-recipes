Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Delicious peanut butter cookies using only 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degress.
  2. Mix ingredients in a bowl using an electric mixer, until smooth and creamy. Roll mixture into 1 inch balls and flatten each with a fork.
  3. Bake until edges are firm, about 10 minutes. Cool on a baking sheet briefly before moving to a wire rack to cool completely.
