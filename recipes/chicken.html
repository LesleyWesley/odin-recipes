Air Fryer Cornflake Chicken Fingers

Description

An air fryer spin on classic cornflake chicken for extra-crispy chicken fingers! Extra delicious when paired with honey mustard.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken tenders
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 cup cornflakes, crushed
  • 1 serving avocado oil cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 375 degrees F.
  2. Season chicken tenders with poultry seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Dip in egg and then coat with cornflakes. Place in air fryer basket and spray with avocado oil.
  3. Air fry for 7 to 8 minutes. Flip, spray with avocado oil again, and air fry for additional 3 to 5 minutes, until 165 degrees F.
